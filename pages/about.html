About

I am Carolina Escobar, a journalist, digital designer and illustrator from Buenos Aires, Argentina.

I hope my work can inspire others to understand that it is important to have a solidarity point of view.

My illustrations are inspired by nostalgic moments and simple but powerfull things like family, friends or a cup of coffe. Also, the brandbook projects and copywriter work are inspired by charity and real diversity in life.

When I am not working, I enjoy singing, chilling with my family and going to bookshops and the cinema with my best friends.
